Project Description

Welcome to becoming a web developer. The goal of this website is to give you, yes YOU a first hand top down look at the core aspects of entering this challenging and satisfying field. Here you will find information on the different jobs a web developer may undertake as well as the core programming/scripting languages that one would need to know to enter this field. I will also provide information on verious online tools and web hosting options that can make your entry into this field a lot smoother. Enjoy! :)
